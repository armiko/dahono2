Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with a diverse range of clients, from individual content creators and VTubers to established corporations and startups looking for legal structuring and management optimization.

How do you handle confidentiality?

Confidentiality is paramount. We sign Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) with all our clients before beginning any work to ensure your data and business strategies remain secure.

Do you offer long-term management contracts?

Yes, we offer both project-based consulting and long-term management retainers. We can tailor our engagement model based on your specific needs and growth stage.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed project fees, hourly rates for legal consultations, and monthly retainers for ongoing management support. Contact us for a custom quote.

Do you help with crisis management?

Absolutely. Our team is trained to handle PR crises and legal disputes swiftly to minimize impact on your brand's reputation and operations.

How do I get started?

Simply contact us through our form or email at contact@dahono.com. We'll schedule an initial discovery call to understand your needs and propose a tailored solution.
